# Product Direction

## Core Idea

MetalChain is a platform where users can learn about gold and silver, practice safe investing habits, and interact with digital representations of precious metals in accessible ways.

## Target Users

- beginners who want to understand precious metals
- users in low-connectivity environments
- users interested in stable, real-world asset exposure
- Web3-curious users who want simpler entry points

## Product Goals

- teach before users trade
- make onboarding simple
- reduce fear and confusion around precious metals
- support low-bandwidth and offline-aware flows
- prepare for future tokenized asset infrastructure

## MVP Features

- education modules
- market overview
- price tracking
- paper trading
- portfolio dashboard
- account and profile basics

## Post-MVP Ideas

- wallet integration
- tokenized gold and silver on Stellar
- offline transaction drafting and later sync
- real seller discovery marketplace
- authenticity and custody verification flows

## Risks

- regulatory requirements for real-asset trading
- trust and custody for underlying metals
- price oracle integrity
- UX complexity from mixing education, trading, and wallets

## Product Rule

If a feature increases regulatory exposure or operational complexity, it should remain simulated until the legal and partnership model is defined.
