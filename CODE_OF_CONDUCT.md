# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in the MetalChain community a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- be respectful and constructive
- assume good intent, while giving clear feedback
- welcome newcomers and different skill levels
- focus critique on ideas, code, and decisions, not people
- share context generously

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment or discrimination
- personal attacks
- trolling or intimidation
- doxxing or sharing private information without consent
- repeated disruptive behavior after warning

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

Until a dedicated reporting channel is created, open a private maintainer contact process before public launch.

If you are the project owner, replace this section with a real contact method before publishing.
