# Governance

## Project Stage

MetalChain is currently maintainer-led.

## Decision Making

In the early stage, maintainers guide:

- roadmap priorities
- architecture decisions
- release readiness
- security-sensitive boundaries

## Contributor Path

Regular contributors can grow into trusted reviewers and maintainers over time through:

- consistent, high-quality contributions
- respectful collaboration
- strong product and technical judgment
- reliable follow-through

## Future Governance

As the project grows, governance can evolve toward:

- clearer maintainer roles
- documented review ownership
- RFCs for major product and architecture changes
